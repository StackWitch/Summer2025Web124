Exercise Questions and Answers

1. Variable to store your full name

Answer: Declared variable fullName storing my full name as a string.

2. Variable to store your desired annual salary

Answer: Declared variable desiredSalary storing a number representing my desired annual salary.

3. Variable to store your veteran status

Answer: Declared variable isVeteran storing a boolean true or false representing veteran status.

4. Array to store the names of three of your friends

Answer: Declared array friendsNames with three friend names.

5. Array to store the desired annual salary for your three friends

Answer: Declared array friendsSalaries with numeric salaries for each friend.

6. Literal object to store a friend's first name, last name, and desired annual salary

Answer: Declared object anotherFriend with properties firstName, lastName, and desiredSalary.
